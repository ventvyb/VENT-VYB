# VENTVYB

> *A private 1-on-1 listening call for men who are feeling low — anonymous, worldwide, pay only if it helped.*

---

## What is VENTVYB?

VENTVYB is a peer emotional support service built for men. It offers anonymous, one-on-one voice calls with a real human listener — no therapy, no advice unless asked, no judgment. The caller pays only if they feel better after the call. If the call didn't help, they owe nothing.

The name comes from two ideas: **venting** (saying what's on your chest) and **vibe** (the feeling of being genuinely heard). That's the whole product.

---

## Who it's for

- **Men only** — a space where men talk to men, without performance or filter
- **English speaking** — all calls are conducted in English; men from any country are welcome
- **Anyone feeling low** — not just crisis situations. Loneliness, stress, anxiety, grief, feeling stuck — all valid

---

## How it works

1. A man books a slot at [cal.com/ventvyb](https://cal.com/ventvyb) — no account, any name
2. He receives a private anonymous call link (via [Whereby](https://whereby.com))
3. The call happens — he talks, the listener listens
4. After the call, he pays what felt right via [Ko-fi](https://ko-fi.com/ventvyb) — or nothing at all if it didn't help

That's the entire model.

## The model

VENTVYB runs on a **pay-what-helped** model. There is no upfront charge. After a call, the listener sends a Ko-fi link. The caller contributes whatever feels right — or nothing, with zero follow-up. This removes the financial barrier entirely and builds trust before asking for anything.

Suggested contribution after a call: $10–$25. Never required.

## Important disclaimer

VENTVYB is **peer support — not a licensed mental health service**. The listener is not a therapist, counselor, or crisis worker. If you or someone you know is in immediate danger, please contact a crisis line. A worldwide directory is available at [findahelpline.com](https://findahelpline.com).
