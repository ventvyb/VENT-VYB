What is VENTVYB

Not therapy.
Just a real human
who will listen.

Sometimes you don't need a diagnosis or a five-step plan. You just need someone to sit with you in it — no advice unless you want it, no clock ticking, no judgment. That's what VENTVYB is. A quiet space where you can say anything.

How it works

Simple as it should be.

1

Book a slot

Pick a time that works for you. No account needed, no personal details required. Just a name — any name — and a time.

2

We talk — privately

You get a private link. We connect anonymously. You talk, I listen. There's no script, no pressure, no agenda. Just a real conversation.

3

Pay what felt right — or nothing

After the call, if you feel even a little lighter, you can contribute whatever feels fair. If it didn't help, or you can't — you owe nothing. Zero.
